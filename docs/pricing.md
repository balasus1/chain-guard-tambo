# Chain-Guard — Pricing Strategy

_Date: June 2026_

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **SMB-first** — Entry price must feel like a no-brainer. A business shipping 50+ packages/month is spending thousands on freight. $29/month is noise.
2. **ROI-anchored** — Every tier should be justified by the cost savings it unlocks. "This plan pays for itself after 1 recovered overcharge."
3. **Usage-based growth** — Plans scale with shipment volume, not seat count. A 2-person team shipping 500 packages should pay more than a 5-person team shipping 50.
4. **No sales calls below $499/month** — Self-serve up to the Business tier.
