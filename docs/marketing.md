# Chain-Guard — Go-to-Market & Marketing Strategy

_Date: June 2026_

---

## Positioning

### One-Line Pitch
"Chain-Guard catches what your carriers hope you'll miss."

### Extended Positioning
Chain-Guard is the AI-powered logistics audit platform for SMBs — tracking every shipment, enforcing every SLA, recovering carrier overcharges, and scoring vendor reliability. No sales call. No 6-month onboarding. Live in 5 minutes.

### What We Are NOT
- Not a freight booking tool (not Flexport)
- Not a route optimizer (not Route4Me)
- Not a WMS (not ShipBob)
- Not just a tracking page (not AfterShip)

We are the **audit + accountability layer** that sits on top of your existing logistics stack.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP — "The Scaling Operator"
- **Who:** Founder or ops lead of an e-commerce or import/export business
- **Company size:** 5–50 employees
- **Shipping volume:** 50–1,000 shipments/month
- **Current pain:** Tracking 10+ carriers across browser tabs, discovering SLA breaches after the customer complains, losing money on invoices they don't audit
- **Budget signal:** Already paying for Shopify, ShipStation, or similar tools
- **Where they hang out:** Shopify community, r/ecommerce, LinkedIn logistics groups, WeMakeDevs, IndieHackers

### Secondary ICP — "The Stretched 3PL"
- **Who:** Operations manager at a small 3PL or freight forwarder
- **Company size:** 10–100 employees
- **Shipping volume:** 200–5,000 shipments/month
- **Current pain:** Managing carrier relationships manually, getting burned on SLA claims, no data to support vendor renegotiations
- **Budget signal:** Already paying for TMS software
- **Where they hang out:** FreightWaves, LinkedIn logistics groups, Supply Chain Now podcast

## Go-to-Market Channels

### Channel 1 — SEO + Content Marketing (Months 1–6)
**Goal:** Own the keywords SMBs search when they feel the pain.

Target keywords:
- "carrier overbilling check"
- "how to audit UPS invoice"
- "shipment SLA tracking software small business"
- "vendor reliability scoring logistics"
- "logistics anomaly detection tool"

Content angles:
- "We audited 1,000 FedEx invoices — here's what we found" (data story)
- "How to hold your carriers accountable (with proof)" (how-to)
- "7 carrier billing errors you're probably missing" (listicle)
- "Why enterprise supply chain tools don't work for SMBs" (positioning)

**Estimated lead cost:** $0–20/signup (organic)

---

### Channel 2 — Product Hunt + Hacker News + WeMakeDevs
**Goal:** Initial traction, early adopter community.

- Product Hunt launch: Position as "AI logistics audit agent for SMBs"
- HN Show HN: Lead with the technical angle — "I built a policy-governed AI agent for logistics"
- WeMakeDevs: The project started here (Week 1 & 2 hackathon) — built-in community
- Dev.to / Hashnode post: "How I built a logistics audit agent with Tambo + Archestra"

**Estimated signups from launch:** 200–500 (spike)

---

### Channel 3 — Shopify App Store
**Goal:** Reach e-commerce operators exactly when they feel the pain.

- Build a Shopify app that auto-creates trackings from orders
- App listing: "Logistics Audit & SLA Tracking for Shopify Stores"
- Free tier drives installs; upgrade path to paid for audit features
- Shopify operators are high-value: they ship daily, they care about customer experience, they already pay for apps

**Estimated lead cost:** $15–50/paid customer

---

### Channel 4 — LinkedIn + Outbound to 3PLs
**Goal:** Close mid-market deals ($79–199/month) faster.

- Target: Ops managers, supply chain leads, logistics directors at 10–100 person companies
- Message: "I found $1,200 in carrier overbilling for a company like yours in 10 minutes. Want to see?"
- Tool: Apollo or Hunter.io for prospecting
- Conversion: Demo → 14-day trial → paid

**Estimated lead cost:** $50–150/paid customer

---

### Channel 5 — Integrations & Partnerships
**Goal:** Get embedded in the existing workflow.

- TrackingMore partner program (already using their API)
- Shopify Partner Program
- Zapier / Make.com connector (extends reach without engineering)
- Freight forwarder communities: CSCMP, FreightTech 25

## Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–3)
- [ ] Build landing page with email waitlist
- [ ] Write 3 SEO blog posts targeting core keywords
- [ ] Create demo video (90 seconds: problem → solution → wow moment)
- [ ] Set up Intercom for live chat + onboarding emails

### Soft Launch (Week 4)
- [ ] Open to waitlist + WeMakeDevs community
- [ ] Gather 20 paid beta users at 50% discount
- [ ] Record customer testimonials / case studies

### Public Launch (Week 6–8)
- [ ] Product Hunt launch
- [ ] HN Show HN post
- [ ] Press outreach: FreightWaves, SupplyChainBrain, TheLoadBoard

### Growth (Month 3+)
- [ ] Shopify App Store listing
- [ ] Content marketing engine (2 posts/week)
- [ ] LinkedIn outbound campaigns
- [ ] Referral program: Give a month free, get a month free

## Competitive Messaging (When Asked)

**vs. AfterShip:** "AfterShip tells you where your package is. Chain-Guard tells you what went wrong, who's responsible, and how much it cost you. Then it handles it."

**vs. FourKites/project44:** "Same visibility, 1/20th the price, no 3-month sales process. You're running a business, not an enterprise procurement cycle."

**vs. Spreadsheets:** "You're already doing this manually — you're just doing it wrong and late. We automate the part that doesn't need a human."

## Brand Tone

- **Direct.** No fluff. State the problem, state the solution.
- **Credible.** Use numbers. "$3,400/year in overcharges" beats "save money."
- **Human.** Talk to a 5-person team, not a Fortune 500 committee.
- **Confident.** Not apologetic about being a startup. Proud of being built lean and fast.
